Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At LA Relocation (Carol Anderson, Realtor®), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide to us when using our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you voluntarily submit forms (e.g., contact forms, home valuation requests).
  • Property Preferences: Information regarding your home buying or selling criteria, location preferences, and budget.
  • Usage Data: Non-identifiable information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system, collected via cookies to improve website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide real estate services.
  • To send you property alerts, market updates, or newsletters (you may opt-out at any time).
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and real estate regulations.

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., CRM platforms, email marketing tools) solely for the purpose of operating our business, under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some site features.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at carol@larelocation.com.
